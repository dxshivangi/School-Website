Enrollment for Fall 2025 is now open. Joining Xavier is straightforward — here's everything you need to know.

Application Process

How to Apply

Our four-step process is simple, transparent, and designed to get your student started quickly.

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with basic family and student information. No application fee required.

2

Provide Records

Upload prior transcripts, standardized test scores, and any relevant academic records from previous schools.

3

Enrollment Call

Meet with our Student Success team to review your student's goals, placement, and personalized learning plan.

4

Welcome Aboard!

Complete enrollment paperwork, set up your portal access, and get ready for your first day at Xavier!

Who Can Enroll

Enrollment Requirements

Xavier welcomes students in grades 6–12 from all backgrounds and learning styles. We accept rolling enrollment throughout the year.

  • Students currently in grades 6 through 12
  • Prior academic records (transcripts from previous school)
  • Commitment to a minimum course load
  • Parent/guardian agreement to our partnership policies
  • Access to a computer and reliable internet connection
  • No entrance exam required for most students

Rolling Enrollment

We accept new students throughout the year — no need to wait for the next semester. Start as soon as you're ready.

All Learning Styles Welcome

Whether your student is a traditional learner, student-athlete, performer, or just needs more flexibility — we adapt to them.

Tuition & Fees

Transparent, all-inclusive pricing with no hidden fees. Payment plans available for all families.

Program Grades Annual Tuition Monthly Plan Included

High School Standard 9–12 $5,800/yr $580/mo Full diploma + 2 electives
High School + AP 9–12 $7,200/yr $720/mo Full diploma + AP courses

Payment Plans

Choose annual, semester, or monthly payment — we work with your family's budget to make enrollment possible.

Sibling Discounts

Enroll more than one child and receive a 10% discount on each additional student's tuition.

Need-Based Aid

Limited scholarships and need-based financial assistance is available. Contact us to learn more and apply.
