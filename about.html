About Xavier

A progressive learning community dedicated to nurturing curiosity, inspiring growth, and preparing students for the future.

Welcome

Welcome to Xavier High School

Xavier High School is a progressive learning community dedicated to nurturing curiosity, inspiring growth, and preparing students for the future.

From Grade 1 to Grade 12, our internationally focused academic programmes encourage students to think critically, explore creatively, and develop the confidence needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

At Xavier High School, education goes beyond the classroom. We focus on building character, resilience, and lifelong learning skills that empower students to reach their full potential.

"Empowering Lifelong Learners | Inspiring Curiosity | Building Resilience"

Inspiring Curiosity

We encourage students to ask questions, explore ideas, and develop a sense of wonder about the world around them. Curiosity is the foundation of innovation and lifelong learning.

Building Resilience

Inspired by the symbolism of the phoenix — representing growth, transformation, and renewal — our school encourages every student to rise to their fullest potential.

Mission Statement

Our Mission

At Xavier High School, our mission is to provide a nurturing and intellectually stimulating learning environment that empowers students to become confident, independent, and responsible individuals.

We strive to inspire curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and cultivate resilience so that our students develop the skills and mindset required to succeed in higher education and in life.

Through strong academic foundations and meaningful learning experiences, we aim to prepare students to become thoughtful global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Empowering Lifelong Learners

Inspiring Curiosity, Nurturing Growth, and Fostering Resilience

Inspiring Curiosity

We encourage students to ask questions, explore ideas, and develop a sense of wonder about the world around them. Curiosity is the foundation of innovation and lifelong learning.

Nurturing Growth

Every child is unique. Our school is committed to supporting the academic, emotional, and personal development of each student in a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Fostering Resilience

We help students develop perseverance, adaptability, and confidence so they can overcome challenges and continue to grow throughout their lives.

At Xavier High School, our vision is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset necessary to become independent learners and responsible global citizens.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

The educational philosophy at Xavier High School is guided by a set of core values that shape the character and growth of our students.

01

Curiosity

We encourage students to ask questions, explore ideas, and develop a lifelong love of learning.

02

Integrity

We promote honesty, responsibility, and ethical behaviour in all aspects of school life.

03

Resilience

We teach students to persevere through challenges, adapt to change, and grow stronger from their experiences.

04

Respect

We value diversity, empathy, and mutual respect within our school community.

05

Excellence

We strive for high standards in academic achievement, personal development, and leadership.

06

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to become thoughtful global citizens who think critically and contribute positively to society.

Founder's Message

A Message from Our Founder

Xavier High School was established with the belief that education should inspire curiosity, nurture confidence, and empower young minds to reach their full potential.

Our vision has always been to create a school where students feel supported, challenged, and motivated to grow — not only academically, but also personally. Education is not simply about preparing students for examinations; it is about preparing them for life.

At Xavier High School, we strive to build a community where students, teachers, and parents work together to support each child's journey of discovery, learning, and achievement.
