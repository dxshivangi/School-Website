Grades 1–4

Primary Years

The primary years focus on developing essential literacy, numeracy, and communication skills while encouraging curiosity and creativity. These years lay the foundation for confident and enthusiastic learners.

Our Primary programme nurtures a love of learning through engaging, hands-on activities that spark imagination and build strong academic skills.

Our Pedagogy

Our Learning Approach

At Xavier High School, learning is designed to be engaging, challenging, and meaningful. We believe that the best education goes beyond the textbook — it develops the whole student.

Assessment is continuous and includes assignments, projects, presentations, and examinations to ensure a well-rounded evaluation of student progress.

Apply Now

Teaching Methods

  • Interactive and discussion-based learning
  • Project-based learning and research
  • Collaborative group work
  • Critical thinking and problem solving
  • Independent learning and inquiry

Continuous Assessment

Assignments, projects, presentations, and examinations ensure a well-rounded evaluation of every student's progress throughout the year.
